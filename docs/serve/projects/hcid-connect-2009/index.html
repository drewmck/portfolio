HCI/d-Connect

Professional-to-student networking events usually involve resume submissions and brief interviews, but rarely involve the employer and potential employees interacting on any deeper level. We wanted to change this with HCI/d-Connect...

Overview

The HCI/d-Connect event was approached as a design problem. The idea was to connect students and employers at a deeper level than simple interviews and resume submissions. We wanted employers to socialize, problem solve and work on small team projects directly with students. Through this the hope was that employers would get a much better understanding of how IU students worked together.

Event Results

The event took place on Jan 22-23rd and was considered a major success. The networking focus of the event connected both students-to-employers as well as employers-to-employers. We are already planning for HCI/d CONNECT '10.
