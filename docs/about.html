About

I'm an interaction designer and iOS developer from Bloomington, IN. In 2011 I founded Bloomingsoft to create user experience-focused iPhone and iPad apps for the consumer and business market. That same year my app Droplist (now Listacular) received front-page treatment from Apple, allowing me to leave my full-time job and go part-time as a freelancer.

My graduate project, DrivingBuddy was acquired by Mentor eData in 2011. In 2012, I launched SpeechHero, an App to help non-verbal kids communicate with the people in their lives.

Prior to Bloomingsoft, I worked for Cook Medical, Disney Animation Studios and Deloitte Consulting. Whenever I'm not working you can find me camping, mountain biking and drinking fine local beers.
